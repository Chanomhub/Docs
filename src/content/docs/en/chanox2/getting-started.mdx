### 1. Choose Theme
Select your preferred theme:
- **Dark Mode** - Dark colors, ideal for night use
- **Light Mode** - Bright colors, ideal for daytime use

## Importing Games

### Method 1: Auto Scan
1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Library**
2. Click **Add Folder** and select your games folder
3. ChanoX2 will scan and add games automatically

### Method 2: Add Manually
1. Click the **+** button in the top right corner
2. Select **Add Game**
3. Choose the game executable (`.exe`, `.app`, or binary)
4. Fill in game info (name, cover image)
5. Click **Save**

### Method 3: Drag & Drop
Drag game folders or executables directly into ChanoX2

## Managing Your Library

### Views
ChanoX2 supports multiple views:
- **Grid View** - Display as a grid with cover images
- **List View** - Display as a list
- **Compact View** - Condensed display

### Organization
Organize your games with:
- **Tags** - Add custom tags
- **Categories** - Categorize by genre (Action, RPG, etc.)
- **Engine** - Group by Game Engine (Unity, Unreal, etc.)

### Search
- Use the **Search Bar** at the top to find games
- Press `/` to open Quick Search
- Filter by Tags, Engine, or Platform

---

## Launching Games

### Normal Launch
1. Click on the desired game
2. Click **Play** or press `Enter`

### Launch Options
Right-click a game for options:
- **Play** - Launch normally
- **Play with Args** - Launch with arguments
- **Open Folder** - Open game folder
- **Properties** - View and edit game info
